Dhaka is Bangladesh's capital and largest bridal makeup market, home to the country's most established and premium artists. For Holud (Holud / Gaye Holud) — the turmeric ceremony, typically held a day or two before the wedding, featuring bright yellow and floral-themed bridal looks — brides in Dhaka typically budget between ৳2,200 and ৳8,800 depending on the artist's experience level, the complexity of the look, and whether hairstyling or draping is bundled in.

Popular areas for bridal makeup studios in Dhaka include Gulshan, Banani, Dhanmondi, Uttara, Mirpur. Artists in these areas tend to charge at the higher end of the range due to demand and overhead costs.

What Affects Holud Makeup Pricing in Dhaka

Key Pricing Factors

• Artist experience & portfolio — established and influencer-tier artists charge 2–4x emerging artist rates.

• Studio location vs. home/venue visit — visiting your venue typically adds a travel charge, especially outside central Dhaka.

• Look complexity — Holud calls for natural, dewy looks in yellow, orange and floral tones — heavy contouring is usually avoided, which affects time and product cost.

• Add-ons — hairstyling, saree/lehenga draping, false lashes, and trial sessions are usually quoted separately.

• Season — peak wedding season (November–February) in Bangladesh sees 15–25% higher rates due to demand.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Holud / Gaye Holud makeup cost in Dhaka?

Holud / Gaye Holud makeup in Dhaka typically ranges from ৳2,200 for emerging artists to ৳8,800 for premium/influencer-level artists, with an established artist averaging around ৳4,000. Prices vary based on the artist's experience, products used, and whether hairstyling or draping is included.

What's included in a typical Holud makeup package in Dhaka?

Most Dhaka artists include base makeup, eyes, lips, and basic hairstyling in their Holud package. Draping, false lashes, and touch-up kits are often charged separately — always ask for an itemised quote before booking.

Should I book a trial before my Holud look in Dhaka?

Yes — a trial session is strongly recommended, especially for first-time clients. It lets you test how the natural, dewy looks in yellow, orange and floral tones — heavy contouring is usually avoided performs in Dhaka's climate and confirms the final look before your big day.

How far in advance should I book a Holud makeup artist in Dhaka?

Popular artists in Dhaka get booked 3–6 months in advance during peak wedding season (November–February). Book early to secure your preferred artist and date.
