Bangladesh's Free Bridal Beauty Planning Toolkit

Built specifically for Bangladeshi weddings — Holud, Akad, Biye and Walima — with local pricing and climate-aware advice.

Planning a wedding in Bangladesh involves multiple beauty-focused functions — the turmeric-themed Holud, the intimate Akad ceremony, the elaborate Biye reception, and the post-wedding Walima — each requiring its own look, budget line, and timeline. Bridal Beauty Hub Bangladesh brings together five free tools so you can plan every function without juggling spreadsheets, WhatsApp screenshots, and guesswork.

Our Makeup Cost Calculator uses real 2024 market rates across Dhaka, Chittagong, Sylhet, Khulna and Rajshahi to estimate your total spend. The Budget Planner tracks actual spending against suggested category splits in Taka. The Beauty Timeline Planner builds a personalised prep schedule counting back from your wedding date, adapted to Bangladesh's hot, humid climate. The Trial Checklist Generator makes sure nothing is missed at your makeup trial, and the Skincare Planner gives you a week-by-week routine suited to tropical weather.
